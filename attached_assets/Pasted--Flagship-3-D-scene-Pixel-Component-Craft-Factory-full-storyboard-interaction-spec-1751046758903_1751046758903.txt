#### Lighting & materials

* **Key light**: area light overhead, cool tint, 60 % intensity.
* **Fill lights**: two rim lights at ±120°, low intensity, warm tint to accentuate edges.
* **Materials**:

  * Conveyor & arms: matte metal with slight roughness variance.
  * UI tiles: emissive surface on front face, glassy rim.
  * Logo morph cube: PBR plastic with programmable emission for face-by-face light-up.

#### Camera & controls

* **Initial path**: GSAP timeline—dolly-in, lateral sway, overshoot + settle.
* **User mode**: Three.js `OrbitControls` (desktop) / custom gyro (mobile).
* Frustum-culled particle emitters to minimise overdraw.

#### Responsiveness & fallbacks

* Device check on first render:

  * ≥8 GB RAM & WebGL2 → full scene, 60 fps.
  * 4-8 GB → reduced shadow maps, lower poly conveyor.
  * <4 GB or WebGL1 → static poster with video loop fallback (compressed 720p H264).
* `prefers-reduced-motion`: skip camera dolly, disable particle rain, auto-show CTA.

#### Accessibility tags

* `<canvas aria-hidden="true">` with adjacent `<figcaption>`:
  *“Animated factory assembling UI components into Petar Nikolić monogram. Press ‘Skip Animation’ to continue.”*
* `Escape` key or on-screen “Skip” instantly jumps to final logo state.
