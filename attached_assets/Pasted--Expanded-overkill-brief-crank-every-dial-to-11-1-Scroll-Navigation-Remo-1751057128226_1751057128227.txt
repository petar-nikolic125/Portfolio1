  body { overscroll-behavior-y: contain; }

## 7. Animations & timing

* **Easings**:

  ```css
  --spring: cubic-bezier(.22,1.61,.36,1);
  --splat: cubic-bezier(.69,-0.28,.38,1.27);
  ```
* **Keyframes**: define at least:

  * `fade-slide-in`
  * `slide-up-fade`
  * `pop-in`
  * `badge-pulse`
  * `arrow-flutter`
* **Durations**: 300 ms → 700 ms, vary per element.
* **IntersectionObserver** to trigger only on first viewport entry.

## 8. Background & polish

* **Subtle grain**: `background-image: url('/grain.png')`, `opacity: .04`.
* **Section backgrounds**: alternating dark tones for visual separation.
* **Glowing radial overlays** behind key elements (Hero heading, featured project) using `::before` pseudo.
* **Subtle parallax**: two background layers move at 10 %/20 % scroll rates.

---

## 9. CSS architecture

* **Utility-first**: Tailwind + custom plugins for animations.
* **Variables**: central theme file for colors, spacings, easings, durations.
* **Container queries**: adjust bullet layout, font sizes, and animation intensity on narrow viewports.

---

## 10. Mobile-first & touch

* **Bullets collapse** to accordions on ≤ 600 px.
* **Tap effects**: use `:active` transforms instead of hover.
* **Section padding**: top/bottom 6 vh.
* **50 % fewer animation frames** on mobile; throttle via JS check (navigator.hardwareConcurrency).

---

> **Ultimate mandate:** bury the page in **killer CSS**, **next-level animations**, and **over-the-top visual polish**—yet keep the flow intuitive, the content realistic, and the layout ruthlessly CV-focused.
